About ReclaimSA

ReclaimSA is a youth-led movement committed to restoring dignity, transparency and opportunity in South Africa through lawful, non-violent civic action, digital campaigns, and policy-driven engagement. This site is the central hub for demands, petitions, volunteer coordination, training and news.

Mission

We mobilise and organise youth to hold public power accountable, restore essential services, and create economic opportunity — using lawful tactics that build broad public support and convert action into policy.

Our Core Demands

1. Immediate transparency: Publish procurement and senior-executive salary audits for top departments within 90 days.
2. Anti-corruption action: Establish an independent anti-corruption task force with prosecutorial authority and whistleblower protection.
3. Restore basic services: Publish and implement a 30/60/90-day public plan to restore electricity, water and healthcare in affected municipalities.
